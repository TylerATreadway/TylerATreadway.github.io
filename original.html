NextGen Gaming

The future of gaming is to put the games on the devices people want and let them run them anytime and anywhere. That means on PCs, mobile phones as well as proprietary console devices.

Gaming platform's like Sony Playstation just started offering a streaming service. Players will no longer need to buy physical games or download them and instead can rent them month by month via a subscription service. Long loading times will be a thing of the past. Next generation consoles use the latest i9 processors and are quantum jumps faster than previous models. Gamers will feel like they are in the game itself.
